ℹ️ About BMI / Category Guide

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using your height and weight. It helps to estimate body fat and categorize your weight status.

Underweight < 18.5
Normal 18.5 – 24.9
▼
Overweight 25 – 29.9
Obese 30 – 39.9
Severe Obese ≥ 40
